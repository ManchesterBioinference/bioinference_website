I am a biomedical data scientist at the University of Manchester, working in exposome-related research and spatial transcriptomics. My background integrates molecular biology, evolutionary biology, and bioinformatics across both eukaryotic systems and bacteria.
I completed my PhD in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, focusing on the evolution of splice sites. I subsequently transitioned to bacterial genomics, transcriptomics, and evolution, with particular emphasis on intrinsic terminators and promoters.
My current research involves analysing large-scale spatial transcriptomic datasets to investigate how environmental and genetic factors shape gene regulation, mutational processes, and phenotypic outcomes.

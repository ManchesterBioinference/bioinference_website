I obtained my MRC DTP in Immunology at the University of Manchester in 2021. Since then, I have specialized in multi-omic analysis pipeline development and deployment, notably for the Human Immunology Accelerator (Lydia Becker Institute) and the Stroke-IMPaCT consortium (Geoffrey Jefferson Brain Research Centre).

Currently, I am a Senior Biomedical Data Scientist at the MRC CoRE in Exposome Immunology, developing AI/ML pipelines to better understand the exposome's impact on health.
